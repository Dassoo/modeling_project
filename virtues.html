Virtues

Chastity

Sexual purity and fedelity to husband or wife during marriage.

Patience

Quality of self-restraint or of not giving way to anger, even in the face of provocation.

Modesty

Behavior that flows out of remembering our true place of service, and does not conceitedly boast about the self, but boasts in God.

Temperance

Emotional restraint or self-control.

Charity

The highest form of love, signifying the reciprocal love between God and man that is made manifest in unselfish love of one's fellow men.

Compassion

Alludes to kindness and sympathy.

Diligence

Careful and persistent work or effort.
