Modeling Project

Virtues and Vices

Works by German painter and engraver Heinrich Aldegrever or Aldegraf (1502–1555, 1558[1] or 1561), German printmaker Sebald Beham, (1500–1550), and unknown Italian Master. Prints are graphical representations of Vices and Virtues, currently owned by Art Institute Chicago.

What is this?

This is the project that me, Federico, and Evelina carried out for the exam in Data Modeling at Ca' Foscari University, Venice. It features a little website with a Mirador viewer and other information about the Virtues and Vices theme.
