Vices

Lust

Disordered desire for sexual pleasure, where sexual pleasure is "sought for itself, isolated from its procreative and unitive purposes."

Wrath

Strong vengeful hatred or resentment.

Pride

High or exalted attitude.

Gluttony

Excessive eating, drinking, indulgence,and greed.

Avarice

Excessive or insatiable desire for wealth or gain.

Envy

Sin of jealousy over the blessings and achievements of others.

Idleness

The state of being lazy and inactive.
